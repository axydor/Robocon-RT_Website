Design

Robocon-RT works with motor drives, which are specific hardware for controlling brushed and brushless motors, that also support the CANopen or EtherCAT protocols. These motor drives make use of the ethernet port for supporting CANopen and EtherCAT protocols. Most single board computers have at least one ethernet port, so the framework is designed to work with a wide range of hardware.

Instead of expensive hardware, developers can use simulation such as PyBullet to create their own real-time robots. Developers, first can test their robots on simulation therefore they can prevent unexpected hardware failures before. It is more easier than working with real hardware.
